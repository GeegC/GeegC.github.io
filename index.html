Anne of Green Gables

L. M. Montgomery
  • Where are the sources of sexism in the book? To what extent, and how, are these challenged or overcome?
  • How is gender represented in the book? Is femininity performed?
  • Is Anne's imagination an asset to her?
  • How could the story of Anne be modernised? Would the plot work if it was set in the present?
  • Does Anne’s character fit with the ‘Manic Pixie Dream Girl’ trope?
  • What is the significance of names and renaming things in the book?

I Hope This Finds You Well

Natalie Sue
  • What parallels/contrasts can be drawn between this book and Interesting Facts About Space?
  • How is the interplay between humour, anxiety, and sadness handled?
  • How does the author handle the themes of what we show others vs what we keep hidden?
  • To what extent would this have been a different book if the protagonist’s heritage had been Canadian instead of Persian?
  • Do you find the main character relatable? Or are you drawn to any other characters in particular?
